NetSeA Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

NetSeA (Network Service Excellence Assistant) is a WhatsApp-based assistant designed to help users interact with service features, guided menus, and support workflows.

Information We Collect

  • WhatsApp identifiers (e.g., your phone number/WA ID) and basic messaging metadata.
  • Message content you send to NetSeA (text and interactive selections such as buttons/lists).
  • Optional info you provide (e.g., location, time window, and issue descriptions).

How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate NetSeA features and respond to your requests.
  • To troubleshoot, monitor reliability, and improve bot performance and user experience.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse of the service.

Data Sharing

NetSeA sends and receives messages through the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta). We may share limited information with service providers that help run NetSeA (for example, hosting, logging, or automated processing services) only as needed to operate the service. We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to operate NetSeA, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies. Retention duration may vary depending on operational needs.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using NetSeA at any time by discontinuing messages.
  • If you want your data removed (where applicable), contact us using the email below.
